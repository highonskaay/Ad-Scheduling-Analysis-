05 — Verdict

Yes, Scheduling is Working.
Here's the Evidence.

Three factors explain the CPI improvement. Only one of them is scheduling — but it's the dominant one.
01
Ad Scheduling — Primary Driver
The same creative went from 3.97% → 7.22% Install Rate with fewer clicks. This is the strongest signal. You can't explain that through ad mix or placement shift — the underlying audience quality changed. Scheduling is restricting delivery to hours when users are more likely to act, not just browse. That's why installs went up 50% while clicks went down 17%.
02
Ad Spend Consolidation — Supporting Factor
Spend shifted from 2 ads (70/23 split) to 1 ad (84%). When budget consolidates on the best performer, blended CPI improves. This is a secondary contributor — it explains part of the campaign-level improvement but not the creative-level conversion jump.
03
Placement Mix Shift — Minor Factor
Feed up 5pp at campaign level. Within the top ad, placement movement is ±3-4pp max. Not a significant driver. Even if Feed converts better than Reels, a 5pp shift doesn't produce a 35% CPI drop. Correlation, not causation.
Ad Scheduling is the primary driver. Continue monitoring Aurangabad, Bhopal & Varanasi.
